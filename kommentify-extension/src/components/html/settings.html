Detailed Feature Guides
Dashboard - Your Command Center

Purpose: Monitor all your LinkedIn automation activities in one place.

What you'll see:

  • Daily/Weekly engagement stats (likes, comments, connections)
  • Monthly usage vs plan limits
  • Live processing progress for active automations
  • Quick action buttons to jump to any feature

Why it matters: Stay informed about your activity levels to avoid LinkedIn restrictions and maximize your networking ROI.

Ô∏è AI Writer - Create Viral Content

Purpose: Generate engaging LinkedIn posts using AI without writer's block.

How to use:

  1. Enter your topic or industry keyword
  2. Click "Generate Topics" to get 5 viral topic ideas
  3. Select a topic that resonates with you
  4. Click "Generate Post" - AI creates a full post
  5. Edit, copy, or schedule for later

Ô∏è Customize: Adjust tone (professional/casual), length, hashtags, and emojis.

Why it matters: Consistent posting builds your personal brand. AI helps you post daily without spending hours writing.

Automation - Engage at Scale

Purpose: Automatically like, comment, share, and follow based on keywords.

How to use:

  1. Add keywords related to your industry (e.g., "AI", "startup", "marketing")
  2. Set daily quota (start with 10-20 for new accounts)
  3. Choose actions: Like, Comment (AI-generated), Share, Follow
  4. Click Start - works in background on LinkedIn tab

Ô∏è Important: Keep LinkedIn open in a tab. Use business hours for natural activity patterns.

Why it matters: Engaging with relevant content puts you on people's radar, leading to profile views, connection requests, and opportunities.

Networking - Grow Your Network

Purpose: Automatically find and connect with your ideal prospects.

How to use:

  1. Enter search keyword (job title, skill, company)
  2. Set filters: 2nd/3rd connections, exclude terms
  3. Set quota (5-15 per day recommended)
  4. Optional: Add personalized connection note
  5. Click Start - finds people and sends requests

Bonus: Enable "Extract Contact Info" to save emails/phones to Leads tab.

Why it matters: A targeted network opens doors to jobs, clients, partnerships, and industry insights.

Import - Target Specific Profiles

Purpose: Import a list of LinkedIn profiles and auto-engage with them.

How to use:

  1. Prepare CSV file with LinkedIn profile URLs
  2. Upload or paste URLs directly
  3. Choose action: Connection requests or Post engagement
  4. Click Start - processes each profile

Use cases: Event attendees, competitor followers, group members, webinar registrants.

Why it matters: Target specific high-value prospects you've identified through research or events.

Limits - Stay Safe

Purpose: Control daily activity to avoid LinkedIn restrictions.

Ô∏è Recommended limits:

  • New accounts (<6 months): 10-15 actions/day
  • Established accounts: 30-50 actions/day
  • Premium accounts: 50-80 actions/day

Ô∏è Delays: Set 30-60 second delays between actions for natural behavior.

Why it matters: LinkedIn monitors unusual activity. Gradual, consistent engagement is safer than aggressive bursts.

Best Practices for Success
  • Start slow: Begin with 10-15 daily actions, increase gradually
  • Use business hours: 9 AM - 6 PM looks more natural
  • AI comments > likes: Comments drive more engagement back to you
  • Be consistent: Daily small actions beat weekly big bursts
  • Monitor your profile: Check for warnings or restrictions
  • Quality over quantity: Relevant connections matter more than numbers
